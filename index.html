About Kupe House


Kupe House Values

K - Kindness

U - Understanding

P - Perseverance

E - Enthusiasm

The ethos and values of Kupe House have developed from the Kupe legend, emphasizing the idea of making a voyage and returning home to share the benefits and value of the findings with others. All Kupe students and staff are committed to this journey, sharing knowledge and skills to improve the house. Kupe is full of explorers, adventurers, and highly motivated young people. There is a strong sense of whānau (family), fostered by hardworking staff and students, all of whom enjoy being part of a warm, caring atmosphere where they are supported and encouraged to strive for excellence. 'A journey of a thousand miles begins with one step' — in Kupe, we take that first step together.
