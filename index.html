Kupe House Values


K - kindness

U - understanding

P - perserverance

E - enthusiasm

The ethos and the values of Kupe House have developed from the Kupe legend, with emphasis given to the idea of making a voyage and returning home to share the benefits and value of the findings with his/her people. All Kupe students and staff are committed to this journey and to sharing knowledge and skills to improve all of Kupe House. This is a house full of explorers and adventurers, a house full of promise and highly motivated young people.
